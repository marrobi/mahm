You can add the prototype to your home screen to make it feel like a native app on your phone. This will provide a more realistic app-like experience compared to using it in your browser.

To install it on your home screen:

  1. Tap the share icon in your browser
  2. Select "Add to Home Screen"

Once added, tap the new icon on your home screen to launch.

After installation, the prototype will open in full-screen mode without browser navigation elements.
