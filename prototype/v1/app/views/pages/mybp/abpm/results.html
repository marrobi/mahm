What is Stage 1 hypertension?

Stage 1 hypertension means your daytime average blood pressure is between 135/85 mmHg and 149/94 mmHg. At this level, your blood pressure is high enough to increase your risk of heart disease and stroke, but can usually be managed effectively with lifestyle changes and medication if needed.
