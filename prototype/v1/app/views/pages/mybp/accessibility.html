Need help using digital services?

We recognize that not everyone can or wants to use digital health services. If you're digitally excluded, My BP ensures you get the same quality of care through:

  • Enhanced face-to-face appointments - More GP time freed up for those who need in-person care
  • Telephone support - Full service available via phone calls
  • Family/carer involvement - Support for family members to help with digital access
  • Practice support - Staff assistance with using the digital tools
📞 Call for digital support: 01234 567890

Screen reader compatibility

My BP works with popular screen readers including:

  • NVDA - Free screen reader for Windows
  • JAWS - Professional screen reader
  • VoiceOver - Built into Mac and iOS devices
  • TalkBack - Built into Android devices

All content includes proper semantic markup and alt text for images.
