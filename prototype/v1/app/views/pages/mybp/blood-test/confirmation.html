Before your appointment

  • Arrive 10 minutes early for registration
  • Bring photo ID and NHS number if you have it
  • Continue taking your medication as normal
  • No fasting required - eat normally
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated
What happens during your appointment
  1. Registration: Check in at reception with your ID
  2. Preparation: Remove any tight clothing from your arm
  3. Blood collection: Quick blood sample from your arm (1-2 minutes)
  4. Aftercare: Apply pressure to the puncture site
  5. Completion: You can leave immediately after

Total time: Usually 5-10 minutes including waiting

Information:

SMS reminder: You'll receive a text reminder 24 hours before your appointment and when results are ready.
