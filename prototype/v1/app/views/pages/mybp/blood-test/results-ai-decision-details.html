How does the system make treatment recommendations?

Our system follows a structured, evidence-based approach:

  1. Analyses your test results against established normal ranges and your personal baseline
  2. Reviews NICE guidelines for hypertension management and treatment pathways
  3. Considers your medical history including current medications and previous responses
  4. Calculates risk-benefit ratio of different treatment options using clinical algorithms
  5. Makes evidence-based recommendations following established clinical protocols
  6. Alerts your GP of all decisions for clinical oversight and approval

All system recommendations are reviewed by healthcare professionals to ensure patient safety and clinical appropriateness.
