Community location measurement

  1. Choose a convenient community location near you
  2. Book a 10-minute appointment slot
  3. Attend your appointment with your smartphone
  4. A trained volunteer will guide you through the measurement
  5. Your results are automatically saved to your My BP record

App-based measurement

  1. Make sure you're in a quiet, comfortable environment
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions to position your finger
  3. Keep still for about 30 seconds during measurement
  4. Your results are automatically calculated and saved
  5. Review your measurement and add any notes
