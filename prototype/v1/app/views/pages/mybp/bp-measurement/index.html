Take an additional blood pressure reading when you want to. We will review this reading and incorporate it into your care plan.

Information:

Optional reading: While we determine when readings are clinically required, you can submit additional readings anytime. These help us make more informed care decisions.

How to measure your blood pressure

Choose your measurement method

  • With your phone (Lifelight™)
  • At home with a monitor
  • At pharmacy or GP surgery

Lifelight™ - measure with your phone

  1. Sit comfortably and place your finger on your phone's camera
  2. Stay still for about 30 seconds while the app measures
  3. The technology detects blood flow changes through your fingertip
  4. Results are calculated using AI and validated measurement algorithms
  5. The whole process takes less than 5 minutes
Start Lifelight™ measurement

Home blood pressure monitor

If you have your own validated blood pressure monitor at home:

  • Use a monitor validated by the British Hypertension Society
  • Take readings at the same time each day
  • Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measuring
  • Take 2 readings, 1 minute apart
  • Record both readings in your My BP app
Information:

You can find a list of validated monitors at bihsoc.org/bp-monitors

Pharmacy or GP surgery

Many pharmacies and GP surgeries offer blood pressure checks:

  • Book a blood pressure check at your local pharmacy
  • Contact your GP surgery to arrange a reading
  • Some pharmacies offer free NHS Health Checks
  • Ask for your reading to be shared with your GP
