Measure your blood pressure now

Use the built-in Lifelight technology to measure your blood pressure using your smartphone camera.

Important: Before you start

Make sure you:

  • Are in a quiet, well-lit room
  • Have been sitting quietly for at least 5 minutes
  • Haven't had caffeine or smoked in the last 30 minutes
  • Have removed any nail polish from your index finger

How to measure

  1. Position your phone flat on a stable surface
  2. Gently place your index finger over the camera lens
  3. Keep completely still for 30 seconds
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions and countdown
  5. Your results will be calculated automatically

How Lifelight technology works

Lifelight uses your smartphone camera to detect subtle changes in blood flow through your fingertip.

  • The camera detects tiny colour changes in your fingertip caused by your pulse
  • AI algorithms analyse these changes to calculate your blood pressure
  • The technology is clinically validated and meets NHS standards
  • No personal data is stored on your device during measurement
  • Results are encrypted and securely transmitted to your NHS record
