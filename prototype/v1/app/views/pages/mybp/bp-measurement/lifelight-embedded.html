Important: Before you start

Prepare for your measurement:

  • Avoid caffeine for 30 minutes before measurement
  • Don't smoke for 30 minutes before
  • Use the toilet before measurement
  • Sit quietly for at least 5 minutes before starting
  • Make sure your smartphone is fully charged
  • Are in a quiet, well-lit room
  • Remove glasses and ensure your face is clean and makeup-free
  • Sit upright and avoid moving during measurement

How to measure using the app

  1. Make sure you're in a quiet, comfortable environment with good lighting
  2. Hold your phone at arm's length facing towards you
  3. Position your face so your left cheek is clearly visible in the camera
  4. Keep completely still and breathe normally for about 30 seconds during measurement
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions and countdown
  6. The system will take 3 readings automatically with short breaks between each
  7. Your results will be automatically calculated, averaged and saved

How Lifelightâ„¢ technology works

Lifelightâ„¢ uses your smartphone camera to detect subtle changes in blood flow through your cheek.

  • The camera detects tiny colour changes (microblushes) in your cheek caused by your pulse
  • AI algorithms analyse these changes to calculate your blood pressure
  • The technology is clinically validated and meets NHS standards
  • Three readings are taken automatically and averaged for accuracy
  • No personal data is stored on your device during measurement
  • Results are encrypted and securely transmitted to your NHS record
