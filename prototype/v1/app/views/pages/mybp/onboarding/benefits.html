Benefits & Privacy

Learn how My BP can help you manage your blood pressure and protect your information.

Information:

My BP is a free NHS service.

How My BP helps you

Our primary objectives are improving clinical outcomes and clinical safety.

Important: Your safety comes first

  • Your safety is our top priority - We'll alert your doctor immediately if there's any concern
  • Better blood pressure care for you - Especially important if you've had difficulty accessing services before
  • More time with your GP when you need it - We handle routine monitoring so your doctor can focus on what matters most
  • Support that works for you - Whether you're comfortable with technology or prefer face-to-face care

What you'll get from My BP

Information: Personal support tailored to you

  • Clear advice about your blood pressure medication
  • Tips for healthy living that work with your lifestyle
  • Support in the way you prefer - phone, text, video, or face-to-face
  • Help in your language if English isn't your first language
  • Reminders to take your medication when you need them
  • Quick access to your doctor if something urgent comes up

Access and support options

My BP works for everyone - we offer different ways to get support:

  • Different ways to connect: Online, by phone, video call, or face-to-face appointments
  • Language support: Help in your language plus sign language interpreters
  • Cultural support: Advice that fits your cultural and religious needs
  • Easy-to-use formats: Large print, audio, or easy-read versions available
  • Community support: Links to local health programmes in your area

How we keep you safe

My BP follows strict safety and quality standards:

  • Fair treatment: Everyone gets the same high-quality care
  • Safety first: Your safety is always our top priority
  • Privacy protection: Your personal information is kept secure
  • Easy to use: Works for everyone, regardless of computer skills
  • Clear explanations: We'll always explain our recommendations
  • Doctor oversight: Your GP stays involved in all your care decisions

Your privacy and data protection

Information:

Your privacy is our priority. We follow strict NHS data protection standards and use your information only to provide your care.

  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely following NHS Digital standards
  • Only you and your healthcare team can access your information
  • We follow GDPR and NHS data protection policies
  • You can request to see or delete your data at any time
  • We never share your data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • All AI agent interactions are logged for clinical governance
Who has access to your My BP data?
  • Your GP and practice team for clinical decision-making
  • Specialists involved in your hypertension care
  • NHS clinical staff providing direct care
  • Authorized NHS Digital teams for system maintenance (anonymized data only)
  • Clinical governance teams for quality monitoring and safety
