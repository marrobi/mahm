Benefits & Privacy

Learn how My BP improves healthcare outcomes and how we protect your information.

Information:

My BP is a free NHS service.

How My BP helps you

Important: Your safety comes first

  • Your safety is our top priority - We'll alert your doctor immediately if there's any concern
  • Better blood pressure care for you - Especially important if you've had difficulty accessing services before
  • More time with your GP when you need it - We handle routine monitoring so your doctor can focus on what matters most
  • Support that works for you - Whether you're comfortable with technology or prefer face-to-face care

What you'll get from My BP

Information: Personalized support for better health

  • Medication advice that's right for you, with clear explanations of why
  • Lifestyle tips that fit your culture, diet, and daily routine
  • Support in the way that works best for you - by phone, video, text, or in person
  • Help in your preferred language with community-specific advice
  • Personalized reminders to help you stay on track with your medications
  • Quick help when something urgent comes up, with fast referral to your doctor

Accessibility and inclusion

My BP is designed to reduce health inequalities by providing:

  • Multiple access methods: Online, voice conversations, telephone support, face-to-face
  • Language support: Major community languages plus BSL interpretation
  • Cultural sensitivity: Culturally-appropriate dietary advice and religious considerations
  • Accessible formats: EasyRead versions, large print, audio descriptions
  • Community programmes: Integration with NHS London "Million Hearts and Minds"

Trustworthy AI principles

My BP adheres to Microsoft's Trustworthy AI principles:

  • Fairness: Reducing health inequalities across all communities
  • Reliability & Safety: Clinical safety as the overriding priority
  • Privacy & Security: Strict NHS data protection standards
  • Inclusiveness: Accessible to all patients regardless of digital literacy
  • Transparency: Clear explanations of AI recommendations
  • Accountability: Human oversight with GP escalation protocols

Your privacy and data protection

Information:

Your privacy is our priority. We follow strict NHS data protection standards and use your information only to provide your care.

  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely following NHS Digital standards
  • Only you and your healthcare team can access your information
  • We follow GDPR and NHS data protection policies
  • You can request to see or delete your data at any time
  • We never share your data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • All AI agent interactions are logged for clinical governance
Who has access to your My BP data?
  • Your GP and practice team for clinical decision-making
  • Specialists involved in your hypertension care
  • NHS clinical staff providing direct care
  • Authorized NHS Digital teams for system maintenance (anonymized data only)
  • Clinical governance teams for quality monitoring and safety
