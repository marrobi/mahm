Data Usage Statement

How we collect, use, and protect your data in My BP.

Data we collect

Types of data collected in My BP
Data type Purpose
Blood pressure readings Monitor your condition and provide recommendations
Medication information Ensure safe and effective treatment
Appointment and test data Coordinate your care
Demographics Personalize care and identify health inequalities

How we use your data

Information: Direct care purposes

  • Generate personalized medication recommendations
  • Monitor your blood pressure trends
  • Schedule necessary health checks and tests
  • Alert healthcare providers to concerning changes
  • Coordinate care between different healthcare services

Anonymized data for improvement

Information:

We may use anonymized data to improve the service and reduce health inequalities. Your personal information is never included.

What does anonymized mean?

Anonymized data means:

  • All personal identifiers are permanently removed
  • It cannot be linked back to you as an individual
  • It's used only for research and service improvement
  • It helps identify patterns that can improve care for everyone

Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update incorrect information
  • Delete: Request removal of your data
  • Withdraw: Stop using My BP at any time
  • Complain: Contact NHS Digital or the ICO

For more information, see the NHS Digital privacy notice.
