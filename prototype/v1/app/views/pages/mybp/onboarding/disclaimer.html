Simulation Disclaimer

Important: SIMULATION ONLY - NOT FOR CLINICAL USE

This is a demonstration system using dummy data only. This tool does not have medical device approval and is not intended for real-world clinical decision-making. All patient data, clinical scenarios, and outcomes shown are simulated for demonstration purposes only.

What this means

Urgent advice: Not for clinical use

  • This is not a real clinical tool
  • Do not use for actual medical decisions
  • All recommendations are simulated
  • No real prescriptions or appointments are made
  • No real clinical monitoring occurs
  • All agent interactions are demonstrations only

Demonstration scope

This demonstration shows how AI technology could support blood pressure care while following Microsoft's responsible AI principles. The demo showcases:

  • AI coordination supporting blood pressure care
  • Three patient situations: Managing existing high blood pressure, monitoring normal blood pressure, or dealing with newly detected high readings
  • Safety first with automatic GP alerts when needed
  • NHS guidelines and approved medication protocols
  • Fair access to care through accessible, culturally-appropriate support

Dummy data notification

Information:

All data shown in this prototype is dummy data created for demonstration purposes. This includes:

  • Blood pressure readings - All simulated using realistic clinical patterns
  • Medication information - Based on NICE guidelines but not real prescriptions
  • Appointment dates and locations - Fictional booking systems and venues
  • Test results - Simulated U&E monitoring and laboratory values
  • Patient demographics - Dummy profiles covering diverse scenarios
  • AI agent recommendations - Demonstrate clinical decision-making logic
  • Escalation scenarios - Simulated emergency and urgent referral pathways

Purpose of this prototype

This demonstration shows the potential for:

  • AI-driven healthcare coordination while maintaining clinical safety
  • Multi-agent workflow orchestration for complex care pathways
  • Health inequality reduction through accessible, culturally-appropriate tools
  • GP workload reduction through safe automation of routine tasks
  • Integration patterns with NHS App design and clinical systems
  • Trustworthy AI implementation in healthcare settings
What happens if I have real health concerns?

If you have any real health concerns about your blood pressure:

  • Contact your GP or healthcare provider
  • Use NHS 111 for urgent but non-emergency advice
  • Call 999 for emergency medical situations
  • Visit your local pharmacy for health advice
