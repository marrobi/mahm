Welcome to My BP

Your AI-powered blood pressure management tool within the NHS App.

Information:

Clinical safety is our number one priority. My BP uses AI technology to support your blood pressure care according to NHS guidelines.

Non-urgent advice: What My BP can help with

  • Help coordinate your blood pressure care using AI technology
  • Support different care needs: ongoing management, routine monitoring, and new diagnosis
  • Provide medication recommendations that follow NICE guidelines
  • Help improve medicine-taking for all patients, especially those in underserved communities
  • Support your GP by automatically flagging when you need clinical review
  • Connect you with community blood pressure monitoring and lifestyle support
How does the AI system work?

My BP uses AI technology to provide different types of support:

  • Overall coordination - Making sure your care is safe and follows clinical guidelines
  • Blood pressure tracking - Helping you monitor your readings
  • Health assessments - Supporting diagnosis and monitoring
  • Lifestyle support - Advice that fits your culture and daily life
  • Treatment decisions - Helping you and your doctor choose the right treatment
  • Medicine management - Optimising your medications safely
  • Health monitoring - Coordinating tests and safety checks
  • Medicine reminders - Helping you take medicines as prescribed
  • Emergency alerts - Immediate help if something urgent comes up

Getting started

Before you can use My BP, we need to:

  1. Explain the benefits and privacy protections
  2. Share important simulation disclaimer information
  3. Show you our comprehensive data usage statement
  4. Get your informed consent to proceed

This helps us make sure you understand how My BP works and that you're happy to use it.
