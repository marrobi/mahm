Welcome to My BP

Your multi-agentic AI hypertension management tool within the NHS App - completely free at the point of use.

Information:

Clinical safety is our number one priority. My BP uses 9 specialized AI agents working together to support your hypertension care according to NICE guidelines.

Non-urgent advice: What My BP can help with

  • Coordinate your hypertension care through specialized AI agents
  • Support three care pathways: known hypertension, routine monitoring, and new diagnosis
  • Provide NICE guideline-compliant medication recommendations
  • Improve adherence for all patients, especially those in marginalized communities
  • Reduce GP workload while maintaining clinical safety through automatic escalation
  • Access community blood pressure monitoring and lifestyle support
How does the multi-agentic AI system work?

My BP uses 9 specialized AI agents coordinated by a central Orchestrating Agent:

  • Orchestrating Agent - Central coordination with clinical safety priority
  • BP Measurement Agent - Community monitoring coordination
  • Diagnosing Agent - ABPM arrangement and diagnostic support
  • Lifestyle Agent - Culturally-appropriate lifestyle interventions
  • Shared Decision-Making Agent - Patient-centered treatment choices
  • Titration Agent - Medication optimization following NICE protocols
  • Monitoring Agent - Safety monitoring and blood test coordination
  • Medication Adherence Agent - Compliance support and tracking
  • Red Flag Agent - Emergency detection and immediate escalation

Getting started

My BP is designed to support patients in three categories:

  • Category A: Known hypertension patients requiring ongoing management
  • Category B: Normotensive patients needing routine surveillance
  • Category C: Newly detected elevated blood pressure requiring diagnosis

Before you can use My BP, we need to:

  1. Explain the benefits and privacy protections
  2. Share important simulation disclaimer information
  3. Show you our comprehensive data usage statement
  4. Get your informed consent to proceed

This process supports our commitment to transparency, accountability, and trustworthy AI principles.
