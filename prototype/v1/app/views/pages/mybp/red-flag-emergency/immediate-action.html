Your blood pressure is dangerously high. Please follow these immediate safety steps.

What you must do right now

Do

  • Sit down and rest immediately - avoid any physical activity
  • Stay calm - try to breathe slowly and deeply
  • Take your usual blood pressure medication if you haven't already today
  • Have someone stay with you if possible

Don't

  • Do not drive or operate machinery
  • Do not exercise or do strenuous activities
  • Do not take extra medication unless told by a doctor
  • Do not ignore these symptoms - they could be serious
Information:

We are automatically arranging urgent medical care for you. A GP will contact you within 2 hours, or sooner if needed. If you have missed medications for >14 days or are pregnant, emergency protocols are activated immediately.

What happens next

  1. Your GP practice has been notified immediately
  2. You will receive a phone call within 2 hours
  3. If symptoms worsen, call 999 or go to A&E
  4. Continue following the safety advice above
