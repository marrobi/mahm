About MAHM

The Multi-Agentic Hypertension Management (MAHM) system is a demonstration of AI-supported healthcare delivery for blood pressure management. This professional dashboard provides healthcare teams with:

  • Practice-level analytics and reporting
  • Health inequality monitoring and intervention tracking
  • AI system performance and safety monitoring
  • Individual patient management and drill-down capabilities
  • EHR integration status and data flow monitoring

Important: Demo system

This is a demonstration system using simulated data only. Not for clinical use.
